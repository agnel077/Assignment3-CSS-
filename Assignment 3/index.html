About Snickers

Snickers is a popular chocolate bar made by Mars.
It's known for its combination of caramel, nougat, roasted peanuts, and milk chocolate.
The bar was first introduced in 1930 by Frank Mars.Apart from its taste, Snickers gained
popularity through its advertising campaigns, often highlighting the "You're not you when
you're hungry" slogan. The bar's mix of sweet, salty, and nutty flavors has made it a beloved
treat for many, providing a satisfying snack. Snickers is everyone's favorite candy.
